ABOUT US

OUR HISTORY

The vision of Shri. Ramchandra Gandhi and Late Laxmanbhai Gandhi the brand introduced its first outlet in Ahmedabad, Gujarat and today, the legacy still continues nationwide. The story of Vadilal can be traced back to the humble Vadilal Soda Fountain outlet that opened in Ahmedabad in 1926. As time passed by, Vadilal became popular for it's authentic, delicious and a wide variety of ice creams with enticing flavours. Today, Vadilal has evolved from a small retail outlet to a modern corporate entity through breakthrough approaches like constant upgradation of technology and customer-oriented services. The company has successfully matched stride with numerous global ice cream giants who have their presence in India today.
